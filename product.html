Frequently Asked Questions

What's Conversational Analytics?
Conversational Analytics refers to the use of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning techniques to analyse human speech and decipher not just the text of what's said, but also its meaning. At its best, CA can tell you when things you care about happen in a conversation, even when it is expressed differently by different customers.
Doesn't AI need a lot of data before I can get any use out of it?
While it's true that all AI systems need a lot of data to be useful, you can get up and running even without a large data set. As your sales process throws off more and more data, the detection and prediction quality improves steadily (from about 70% accuracy towards 90% accuracy). However, even from the very outset, you'll achieve orders of magnitude improvements in your visibility to the sales funnel.
How does all this work?
We analyse the customer-rep interaction and tell you how it's going both at the level of individual leads but also at an aggregate team level. That helps you share insights on what's working and align the team to best practices more effectively.
Is this a CRM?
salesken is not a CRM. Its AI driven platform enables you to analyse and connect your customer conversations with your leads across the pipeline. That lets track how well or badly those conversations are going, and where your reps need to tune their communication more effectively to convert leads more effectively.
Do I need a CRM to use salesken?
salesken offers simple lead and workflow capabilities out of the box but also integrates with most of the leading CRMs out there. For most basic lead management and tracking, our out of the box capability should suffice
My dialer already records my calls - why do I need this?
Dialers record calls, but who's going to listen to them? Also, you need to understand conversations in the context of your product, your processes, your leads and your pipeline. That's the gap that salesken is filling in
Isn't this complicated to implement?
salesken can be introduced in a staged manner that integrates cleanly with your existing sales technology stack. For evaluation, we have an out of the box solution that just works and doesn't require anything from you except the leads you want to chase down!
We haven't invested in CRM or dialers yet - can we still use salesken?
Sure! If you haven't made any investments in your sales tech stack yet, salesken comes with some plug and play integrations that you can get moving with right from the get go.
